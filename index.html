2019 Technical Workshop on Nuclear Fuel Cycle Simulation

University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign

The 4th annual Technical Workshop on Fuel Cycle Simulation (TWOFCS19) will be held June 26-28, 2019 at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. It is hosted by the Advanced Reactors and Fuel Cycles group in the Department Nuclear, Plasma, and Radiological Engineering.
